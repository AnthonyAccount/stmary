Pastoral Visit

The 'Pastoral Visit' of His Excellency, Most Rev. Romulo G. Valles, D.D., Archbishop of Davao, from June 17 to June 19, was a momentous event showcasing the church's profound impact on the community. Archbishop Valles emphasized the vital role of elders in guiding and loving the youth, reinforcing the church's mission. To the youth in ministry: keep going, stay strong, and remember your why – your love for Jesus and desire for others to know Him.
